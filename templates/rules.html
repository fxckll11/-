📘 12 English Tenses and Conditionals

Present Simple
Subject + V(s/es)
She works every day.
Present Continuous
am/is/are + V-ing
He is reading now.
Present Perfect
have/has + V3
I have finished my work.
Present Perfect Continuous
have/has been + V-ing
She has been studying for 2 hours.
Past Simple
V2
I visited London.
Past Continuous
was/were + V-ing
They were playing football.
Past Perfect
had + V3
She had left before I came.
Past Perfect Continuous
had been + V-ing
He had been working all day.
Future Simple
will + V
I will call you.
Future Continuous
will be + V-ing
She will be working tomorrow.
Future Perfect
will have + V3
I will have finished by 5 PM.
Future Perfect Continuous
will have been + V-ing
He will have been studying for 3 hours.
Zero Conditional
If + Present Simple, Present Simple
If you heat water, it boils.
First Conditional
If + Present Simple, will + V
If it rains, I will stay at home.
Second Conditional
If + Past Simple, would + V
If I were rich, I would travel the world.
Third Conditional
If + Past Perfect, would have + V3
If I had studied, I would have passed the exam.
